How Flaro works

Own a piece of the future through secure investments

Code as Electricity

We've transformed code into electricity - on every run, users pay for it. Every contribution is kind of like a micro-saas or a paid API call. The end product is the total sum of all contributions.

Transparent payouts

We create a huge dependency network of the work to be done in order to reach the goal. Tokens are only staked forward to the next set of tasks to be evaluated, but payments are only released if the investor chooses to do so(read more).

Only proven business

You stake capital to evaluate the nodes of the software being built, but actual payments are only unlocked incrementally on every sale.

Full visibility at all times

Usually, startups do not provide sufficient information about their operations, financial health, or strategic plans. This lack of transparency makes it difficult to assess the true state of the company. On OpenSpur, everything is in the open at all times and recorded on the blockchain.
